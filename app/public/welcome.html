How to Play

Welcome to Freaktures, where you can get your very own freaky-creatures to train and grow. You can randomize a Freakture every two minutes. Remember you can only have one at a time, so saving another Freakture gets rid of your old one!

Freaktures can come in many different shapes and sizes, with different sprites, mutations, types, stats, moves and rarities. There are 7 different rarities you can get: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Mythical, Divine, and The One and Only. They have a chance to improve their rarity as they level up though, so don't give up on one that starts out low!

A Freakture can have up to four moves, unlocking each every 20 levels. The higher the rarity, the better chances for stronger moves. Once they have a full moveset, every five levels gives you a reroll, which allows you to change one of your moves.
